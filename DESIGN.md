# DESIGN.md

This site uses a Vercel-inspired design direction from the `awesome-design-md` collection:

- Vercel — "Black and white precision, Geist font"
- Source collection: https://github.com/VoltAgent/awesome-design-md

## Visual Direction

- Minimal and precise
- High-trust and technical
- Strong typography hierarchy
- Very restrained color usage
- Mostly monochrome with a subtle blue accent
- No heavy gradients
- No card soup
- No loud startup-marketing visuals

## Palette

- Background: `#ffffff`
- Surface: `#ffffff`
- Surface subtle: `#fafafa`
- Border: `#e8e8e8`
- Strong border: `#d9d9d9`
- Primary text: `#0a0a0a`
- Muted text: `#5f6368`
- Faint text: `#8b8f94`
- Accent: `#0f62fe`

## Typography

- Font family: `Inter`
- Headlines should be tight, bold, and compact
- Body copy should be direct and quiet
- Avoid decorative typography

## Layout

- Wide whitespace
- Tight content width
- Strong section rhythm
- Simple grid structure
- Rounded corners are acceptable, but restrained

## Components

- Primary buttons: dark fill, white text
- Secondary buttons: white fill, dark text, thin border
- Cards: white or very light surface, thin border, soft shadow only
- Navigation: minimal, quiet, top-aligned

## Do Not

- Do not use glossy gradients
- Do not use excessive shadows
- Do not use multiple accent colors
- Do not make it feel like a generic agency template
- Do not over-explain with too much copy

## Outcome

The site should feel like:

- a serious technical operator
- a trusted senior builder
- someone who works on high-value problems
- not a loud personal brand
