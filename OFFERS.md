# Offers

This file is the internal version of the public site offers.

## 1. AI Engineering Guardrails Audit

### Best for
- AI-native startups
- founders with small engineering teams
- teams using Cursor, Codex, Claude, or similar tools heavily
- teams where output is rising but judgment is getting weaker

### Problems it solves
- weak review discipline
- inconsistent code quality
- architecture drift
- unclear AI usage boundaries
- juniors shipping faster than they can evaluate

### What the client gets
- codebase and workflow review
- engineering standards recommendations
- AI usage guardrails
- review checklist
- ADR suggestions
- prioritized risks and next steps

### Outcome
- less noise
- clearer standards
- better technical decisions
- a safer path to moving quickly

### Commercial shape
- 2-week audit
- typical price: `£2k-5k`

## 2. Fractional Technical Lead

### Best for
- founders without strong in-house senior technical leadership
- teams between MVP and scale-up mess
- teams needing part leadership, part execution

### Problems it solves
- poor prioritization
- architecture indecision
- unclear ownership
- blocked delivery
- weak technical direction

### What the client gets
- regular senior technical leadership
- roadmap and architecture support
- direct execution on hard problems
- decision-making support
- hiring and quality bar support

### Outcome
- cleaner focus
- better technical coverage
- fewer expensive mistakes
- stronger execution confidence

### Commercial shape
- monthly retainer
- typical price: `£2k-6k/month`

## 3. Workflow / Platform Rescue

### Best for
- product teams with one painful platform area
- expert businesses with broken internal tooling
- teams stuck with recurring systems mess
- teams with ugly internal processes tied to technical systems

### Problems it solves
- bad internal workflows
- fragile tools
- manual rework
- delivery rescue needs
- weak operating procedures
- broken or unclear process around important technical work

### What the client gets
- scoped technical cleanup
- automation or internal tool improvements
- policies, runbooks, or procedures where needed
- hands-on implementation
- process improvement where the workflow itself is part of the problem

### Outcome
- less friction
- more stability
- less heroics
- a usable system instead of a recurring headache

### Commercial shape
- scoped project or short retainer
- typical starting point: `£2k+`

## Best first outreach angle

Lead with:
- clear painful situation
- small-team fit
- direct senior help
- no heavy consulting theater

Do not lead with:
- generic freelancing
- “I can build anything”
- broad AI transformation language
